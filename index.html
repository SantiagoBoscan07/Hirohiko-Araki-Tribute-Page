Hirohiko Araki

Famous writer and artist from Japan.

Hirohiko Araki is a mangaka who is famous for his long-running series "Jojo's Bizarre Adventure," which was first published in 1987 and it continues to produce more chapters to this day. Araki's style is a mix of multitple western elements, such as, rock music, fashion and movies, all of which Araki is a huge fan of. In 2012, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure received an anime adaptation that resulted in the series gaining popularity outside of Japan. Many fans consider Jojo's Bizarre Adventure as one of the most influential mangas of all times, alongside with other classics like Dragon Ball and One Piece.
